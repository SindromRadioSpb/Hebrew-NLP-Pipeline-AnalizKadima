# IAHLT Platform

## Обзор
- **URL:** https://www.iahlt.org
- **Тип:** Non-profit / Membership
- **Цена:** Open resources + Paid membership
- **Категория:** Hebrew NLP platform

## Описание
Israeli Association of Human Language Technologies. Creates annotated datasets for Hebrew/Arabic. UD treebanks, NER corpora. Automated + manual tagging tools. Quality assurance.

## Сильные стороны
- Hebrew-specific expertise
- UD treebanks (IAHLTwiki, IAHLTknesset)
- NER corpora
- Quality assurance management
- Automated tagging + manual review
- Open resources
- Inter-annotator agreement

## Слабые стороны
- No term extraction tool
- No corpus management platform
- Limited public access (membership)
- No association measures
- No TM integration
- Research-oriented, not product

## Уроки для нашего продукта
Hebrew-specific annotation methodology. Quality assurance workflow. Inter-annotator agreement. Open+membership model.
